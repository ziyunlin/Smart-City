THE TECHNOLOGY TRANSPORTATION

GREEN TRANSPORTATION

Barcelona has a good technology transportation. Barcelona attaches great importance to the role of the Internet of Things for smart cities, covering a large area of wireless sensors and routers, these devices generate a lot of data every day, these data flow to the open software platform, further data collection and analysis on the platform, For the city to provide more effective operation guidance.

Barcelona promotes the use of electric vehicles, the deployment of charging stations in the city, as well as green transport facilities such as electric vehicle fleets and car leasing. By 2012, the city has more than 500 hybrid taxis, 294 public electric cars, 262 charging stations, 130 electric motorcycles and about 400 private electric vehicles.



SMART TRANSPORTATION IN SINGAPORE

GARBAGE COLLECTION AND PROCESSING

THE TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION

As early as 2008, the Transport Department of Singapore has installed a large electronic display in the center of the city, listing nearby parking lots and the number of berths available in real time to facilitate motorists to prearrange travel and determine their parking location. The real-time parking data has also been accessible through the mobile app, very convenient and practical. Studies in the United States have shown that up to 30 percent of vehicles on the road during rush hours are looking for parking spaces.

Garbage disposal has always been a major topic of social health. Singapore imported the intelligent garbage collection and processing system. When the household dumps the rubbish into the refuse chute, the waste is transported by high-pressure gas through the underground pipeline to the central treatment collection station at a speed of 50 to 80 kilometers per hour, and then transported to the garbage incinerator by a large garbage truck. Staff reduction of 70%, and also save the needs of the garbage room.

Barcelona to promote technological innovation, in the city high-tech center, opened up an area of about two square kilometers of the block, devoted to research and practice. The old city transformation program uses a series of intelligent technology means, including the promotion of free electric vehicle charging facilities, smart sensor garbage collection point, intelligent sensor parking garage and parking management, and public water management and water conservation plan.
