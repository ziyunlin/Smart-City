WHAT IS SMART CITY?


These are types of cities that is able to optimize resources to increase sustainability and be nature friendly. Smart cities are built around a network of people and cloud based information systems and also leverage the latest technology for the wellbeing of the people.

WHY SMART CITY?

More than Smart economy, smart healthcare and so many aspects that make a city smart, the major problem every city and every human who live in it face is the need of electricity. Most cities in the world produce electricity using finite resources such as coal and petroleum. MuniSkies is determine to increase awareness of this problem and implement the smart city dream toward energy to make the world a better place.
