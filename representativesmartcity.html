The Smart City Singapore:Singapore's beautiful urban environment and high technology make the city become one of the most livable cities in the world, and it is also known as the smartest city in the world. Garbage disposal has always been a major topic of social health. Singapore imported the intelligent garbage collection and processing system. When the household dumps the rubbish into the refuse chute, the waste is transported by high-pressure gas through the underground pipeline to the central treatment collection station at a speed of 50 to 80 kilometers per hour, and then transported to the garbage incinerator by a large garbage truck. Staff reduction of 70%, and also save the needs of the garbage room. TO TOP




The Smart city Barcelona : Barcelona is hailed as a model of the European smart city. As early as 2009, Barcelona City Council has proposed a "smart city" model of the idea, hoping to improve the welfare of citizens and quality of life. By 2012, Barcelona has completed a series of the fruitful wisdom of the city project and was named the European smart city benchmark. Barcelona has a good technology transportation. Barcelona attaches great importance to the role of the Internet of Things for smart cities, covering a large area of wireless sensors and routers, these devices generate a lot of data every day, these data flow to the open software platform, further data collection and analysis on the platform, For the city to provide more effective operation guidance.TO TOP




Smart City ShangHai : Smart city in shanghai main areas of concern include the smart environment, smart water, smart health, smart tourism, electric vehicles and smart grid.People in Shanghai have long-distance medical examination of the hospital, community services for the elderly alone IPTV, can be found in the public can repair electrical appliances to the public cloud, pick-up clothes dry cleaners ... but only just started.Smart city in shanghai is based on fiber-optic broadband network coverage, home fiber-to-the-home and broadband infrastructure. From 2014, Shanghai is ready to create itself as a "livable, innovative, reliable and efficient" smart city, as of the end of this year, Shanghai has basically completed the action plan clear objectives and tasks.TO TOP




Smart city Toronto : Toronto recognizes the importance of a smart city in practice, and IBM recently opened a business analytics solution center in Toronto. Toronto is also an active member of the mega city in Clinton 40 (C40) and is looking for ways to transition to a low carbon economy. In the Toronto private sector, to create a "smart commute to Toronto" initiative, hoping to improve transport efficiency in the metro area. Toronto has also recently begun to use gas-powered, urban garbage trucks and landfills, which are intelligent recycling ideas.TO TOP




Smart city London : London's innovation in the sustainable development and strong transport system have been recognized by everyone. London will soon set up the Smart City Research Center at Imperial College to make the city more efficient and innovative by taking full advantage of traffic, government, business, academia and consumer data. Just a few days ago, London announced its partnership with O2 to launch Europe's largest free Wi-Fi network.TO TOP




Smart city Tokyo : Last year, the city announced plans to create a smart city suburb. In cooperation with Matsushita, Accenture, and Tokyo Gas, it will build a home that incorporates all the solar panels, batteries, and energy efficient appliances that connect to the smart grid. Tokyo will also focus on promoting intelligent mobile solutions.TO TOP




Smart city Hong Kong : Hong Kong is experimenting with RFID technology at the airport and throughout the agricultural supply chain. Hong Kong is already a leader in the use and adoption of smart cards, and millions use smart cards for residential services such as public transport, library access, buildings, shopping malls and car parks.TO TOP
